Culotté.e.s is a pop-up publishing house showcasing copies of student's publication opening space for conversation, critique, and inspiration. Read More...

Culotté.e.s is a student-initiated project founded by Alessia, Maya, and Alex. As students ourselves, we noticed how many thoughtful and creative publications are made with care, only to end up forgotten on a shelf.

This platform was born from a desire to change that.

Culotté.e.s isn’t just a call for submissions — it’s a space to celebrate, archive, and showcase student work. It offers an opportunity for your publication to be seen, shared, and even sold — giving each project the visibility and value it deserves.

Have a look at the submitted publications on our shelf in the Graphic Design Department.

How do I submit my Publication?

step.1 — Choose a Publication

Select one of your publications that you feel is most representative of your work. The panel will meet to review all submissions and select one to be printed, considering aspects like:

  • Strong design choices
  • Originality and clarity of concept
  • Emotional or sensory impact
  • Cohesion between visuals, text, and format
  • Craftsmanship and quality of execution

step.2 — Write a short description

Provide a brief description of your chosen publication - what it's about and any details you'd like to share about the concept.

step.3 — submit

DM us on Instagram both the description and an image of your publication.

step.4 — what happens next

Our panel will review all submissions and select one publication to be printed and sold at Page No Found. All submitted works will also be ar chived and showcased on our shelf, website, and Instagram. It will also contain you contact details so people can reach out directly.

How do I become a panel Memeber?

step.1 — role of the panel

The panel changes each round so that everyone gets a chance to take part. The panel's role is to meet once per round, review all the submitted publications, and choose one to be printed. The decision will be based on how relevant the publication is to the theme, the design choices, and the overall feel of the work.

step.2 — How to apply

DM us on Instagram if you'd like to be part of the next panel.

step.3 — Heads up

If you're on the panel for a round, you won't be able to submit your own work during that same round.
